WHICH TAKEAWAY SHOULD I GET?

A simple heuristic for people who want to order food but aren't sure which delivery or takeaway to get.

Because sometimes you are so uninspired and out of touch with your bodily desires you need a computer to understand them for you.

This website was built with common UK cuisines in mind, I have no idea what you can get in other countries.
